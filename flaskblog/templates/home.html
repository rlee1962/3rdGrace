Why

I named my website 3rdGrace.com because I believe in the simplicity of the Christian Gospel. I've noticed that sometimes, people - even leaders in the church - can make it seem really complicated, as if you need some sort of advanced degree just to understand it. But I don't think it should be that way.

In my view, the Gospel is about God's grace, his kindness that we don't earn but that he gives us anyway. And it's such a simple, beautiful message that even a third grader should be able to understand it. That's where the "3rd" in 3rdGrace comes from.

I named my site 3rdGrace.com to reflect my commitment to make the Gospel clear and accessible to everyone, just like it was intended to be. No advanced degrees needed, no complicated jargon â€“ just the pure, simple message of God's grace.
